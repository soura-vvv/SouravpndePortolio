My Projects

SPECTRAL EQUALIZER

I have always been fascinated about equalizers.I believe equalizers play a crucial role in music,wherein one can tweak the audio spectrum in order to get the desired sound.Spectral equalizer is a three-band equalizer(Low, Peak, High) with a spectrum analyzer, which allows the user to filter audio as per needs.The low and high pass filters are based on infinite impulse response high order Butterworth filters. A Fast Fourier transform is used for the spectrum.Further, I plan on making this equalizer dynamic.By implementing this project I learnt the functioning of filters and fourier transforms.
MAX MODULATION

A tangible user interface that controls audio parameters in Max MSP using the open sound control protocol when moved around in the field of view of the camera.
DELAY

Delays have always helped me make my mixes sound fuller and deeper. It adds that sense of depth to a track. This simple delay implementation helped me understand concepts related to audio buffer and audio feedback.
Synthesizer

Synthesizers form the core of any electronic music today.There are various types of synthesizers around,each serving a different purpose.I developed this basic synthesizer comprising of a single osciallator with different waves like sine,square,saw.The oscillator's envelope can be altered using the ADSR sliders.Further I plan to add a filter can be used to highlight a particular sound or drive out the unwanted sounds.
SAMPLERX

A sampler for me is a very useful tool, as I always try and give a hint of the singer’s voice, either in the intro or the drop but not making it too obvious.Chopping up vocal snippets and pitching them up or down in order to create a melody.This Sampler lets users drag and drop audio and plays it in various pitches.The user can also modify the Attack,Decay,Sustain,Release of that audio.
METRONOME

A Metronome is that one thing, which every musician would have used. I think a metronome is the most basic necessity for any musician.This application comes with a play/pause button, and a beats per minute slider.I learnt the relationship between the samplerate,the bpm, and time intervals.

My Music

One of my recent singles, Pull me closer starts out slowly. My first song where I chopped up vocals in order to fill the drop. The vocalist’s delivery is very energetic and brings out life to the track.
Rather do was a well-thought-out production. I like how it develops through the track. The main driving force of the drop is a pluck which was made using one of the chops from the vocal stacked with a variety of instruments. Since the main pluck is based on the vocalist, it gels up well and the drop doesn’t sound like it came out of nowhere.
Every year my college has a huge festival called “Gandharva”. Every year we have a unique theme based on which, a song is written and produced. It is revealed at the opening ceremony of the festival. That year’s theme was ”Bollywood” so we tried to make something close to the Bollywood trend at that time. I was the co-producer with two other producers. Further, I was in charge of the mixing and mastering of the track. It was a great experience working with such talented writers, singers, and producers. I learned a lot from them as this was my first time working on a song in a team.
This year the theme was “Incredible India”. The inspiration for this song came from all of the diversity in India. We had parts of songs in different languages which exhibited how this country even with such a variety of cultures, still manages to stay together. I had more fun in this process than last year as I was given more artistic freedom by the organizers. We faced issues while incorporating a variety of languages, and singing styles. The overall mix turned out very good and I realized how my mixing skills had gotten a little better over the year.
This track came to life when I was checking out vocal packs online. At that time I was listening to a lot of music by “Kygo”, mostly tropical house and that is what inspired me to use elements like the flute, tambourine, and bongos. I tried to make the drop not too intense but with enough energy to make someone groove.
Back in June 2018, Skio Music announced this new remix contest for Bonnie Mckee’s “Mad Mad World”. I really liked the vocals of this song and already had an idea about how I could flip it up and make a remix out of it. The song turned out quite good but I lacked the mixing and mastering skills, so winning the competition was way too ambitious. However, a lot of critics said that the production was good but the mixing needs a lot of work. Working on a remix was quite an experience and I learned a lot about how one will have to alter the productions in order to make the vocal and other elements fit.
